ENTRYDELL S.A.

ENTRYDELL S.A. was founded in May 1998 in Switzerland.
The core activity of ENTRYDELL S.A. is wholesale of
medical equipment as well as realization of «turn-key»
investment projects in Healthcare and Pharmacology.

ENTRYDELL S.A. offers high-quality equipment and
advanced technologies from leading West-European,
Japan and Korean manufacturers.

The company offers high-end equipment, develops
unique technological solutions and offers perfect
aftersales service and maintenance.

All service engineers were trained and manufacturers’
facilities and got corresponding certificates.
